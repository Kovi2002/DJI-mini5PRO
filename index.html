About

Aerial storytelling,
engineered precisely

I'm a master's student in Electrical Engineering with a robotics specialisation at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Ljubljana (UL FE) — and a drone videographer with a passion for capturing the world from above.

My main tool is the DJI Mini 5 Pro: compact, powerful, and built for cinematic outdoor shots. From sweeping landscapes to architectural details, I combine technical precision with a filmmaker's eye.

Whether you need promotional content, real estate aerials, or event coverage — I bring a methodical, engineering-minded approach to every flight.
